## Overview

Created after foster kittens discovered the TV's power cable. The cover arches over the plug, blocking curious paws from unplugging it — protecting both the kittens and the TV.

The arch is sized to straddle a standard plug up to 27mm tall. Fusion 360 source files are included so the dimensions can be adjusted to fit other plug profiles.
