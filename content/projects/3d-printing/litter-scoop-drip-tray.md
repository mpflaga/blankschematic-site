## Overview

A scoop for the scoop. The litter scoop's wire tines do a great job in the box, but anything small enough to fall through will dribble across the floor on the way to the toilet. This printed tray cradles the litter scoop underneath and catches what falls through, keeping the floor clean.

Inspired by foster kitten litter box cleanups.

## Design Details

The grip is rounded so that small hands can comfortably hold both the litter scoop and the printed tray together at the same time. The geometry causes them to self-align when gripped.
