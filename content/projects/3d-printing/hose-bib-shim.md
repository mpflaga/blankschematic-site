## Overview

A set of shims for use with exterior hose bibs (sillcocks) that are recessed into side-wall paneling. The shim spaces the bib away from the wall surface to restore a flush or proud mount after cladding is added or replaced.

Several depths are provided to accommodate different paneling thicknesses and recess depths.

Based on [Sshelb8](https://www.thingiverse.com/sshelb8/designs).

## Removable Wedge

The lower half includes a snap-away wedge. It can be:
- Left in place for a standard install
- Snapped off permanently where it would interfere
- Temporarily removed and replaced to allow retrofitting around existing pipe

## UV Exposure

Exterior use means UV exposure. Either:
- Print in a UV-stable material (PETG, ASA, or UV-stabilized PLA)
- Cover the shim with exterior paint or caulking after install
