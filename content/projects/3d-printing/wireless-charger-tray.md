## Overview

A wireless charging cradle for the Samsung S10 and S22, designed to sit on a nightstand. The cradle geometry guides the phone to self-align with the charger's antenna when dropped in — solving the common problem of waking up to a phone that spent the night just barely out of alignment.

## Charger

Uses an inexpensive PCB wireless charger module:

<img src="/images/projects/wireless-charger-pcb.jpg" alt="Wireless charger PCB" style="width:25%; transform:rotate(-90deg); display:block; margin:1rem 0;" />

A lip on the underside creates a shelf that conceals the Micro-USB connector flush with the base.

## Customizing

Fusion 360 source files are included for adjusting phone or charger dimensions to fit other devices.
