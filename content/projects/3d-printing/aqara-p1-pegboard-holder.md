## Overview

A printed holder for the Aqara Motion Sensor P1 that clips onto standard US 1" × ¼" peg board. It provides a quick and easily repositionable mount — useful for experimenting with sensor placement before committing to a permanent install.

## Print Orientation

Print right-side up. This orients the layer lines to wrap around the P1 in the XY plane, which provides the most strength where the clip stress is highest.
